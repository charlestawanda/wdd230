Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

The site is named YourTown Chamber of Commerce. This name represents the local chamber's commitment to serving its community and promoting local businesses.

Site Purpose

The site serves two main purposes:

  • To act as a central hub for information about local businesses and events.
  • To facilitate membership for local businesses, providing resources and networking opportunities.

Scenarios

Here are some questions that potential visitors might ask:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

The selected colors for the site are:

  • Primary Color: #0056b3 - Used for headings and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #f8f9fa - Used for backgrounds and paragraphs.

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif - Used for all headings.
  • Body Font: Times New Roman, serif - Used for paragraph text.
